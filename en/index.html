Frequently asked questions

How much do your services cost?

It depends on the task. The turnkey starter package costs €590 and includes a website, Google, domain, email, analytics and your first ad budget. Ongoing work is discussed after a free audit, once the scope is clear.

I already have a website. Can you do promotion only?

Yes. We start by auditing what you already have: website, social media, ads. Then we take on only the areas that will bring results. We don't push you to redo everything.

Do you work outside Estonia?

Yes. We work remotely with clients in the EU, Canada and the US. All communication is online: calls, messengers, reports.

How fast will I see results?

Google Ads brings the first impressions and clicks within the first week after launch. Social media and SEO are a long game: steady growth usually shows in 2-3 months. During the audit we'll tell you honestly what to expect in your niche.

What is the free audit and what do I get?

We review your website, social media and Google visibility and send you a short breakdown: what's blocking new customers and where to start. No strings attached: the breakdown is yours either way.

Do I need to sign a long contract?

No. We work month to month, without long commitments. If the results don't satisfy you, you can stop at any time. Everything is official, with an invoice from an Estonian company.
