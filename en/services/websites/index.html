You have a website but no leads. Why?

A beautiful website is not the same as a working website. Leads are usually lost for three reasons.

Unclear offer

Visitors don't understand what you offer, who it's for and why they should choose you.

Slow and awkward on mobile

More than half of visitors come from phones. If the site lags or breaks, they leave.

No path to an inquiry

A person is ready to reach out but can't see where to click: the form is hidden, contacts are at the very bottom.
