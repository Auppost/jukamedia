Why stores don't sell

Opening a store is easy. Making people buy in it is harder.

Awkward catalog

Products are hard to find, photos are small, descriptions are missing. The buyer goes to a competitor.

No trust at checkout

Unclear delivery and return terms, a suspicious payment page. The cart gets abandoned.

Nobody runs the store

Products aren't updated, prices are stale, orders get lost. Such a store dies within a couple of months.
