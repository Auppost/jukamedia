Where ad budgets get lost

Google Ads works. Money is usually lost not in the channel but in the setup.

Too-broad keywords

Ads run on generic words and pay for clicks from people who wanted something else.

No negative keywords

Nobody filtered out searches with free, DIY, jobs. The budget burns on non-buyers.

No tracking

Nobody knows which ad brought the call, so nobody knows what to switch off.
